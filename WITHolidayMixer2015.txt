This year's PDX Women in Tech (https://twitter.com/PDXWiIT) holiday party was hosted at Puppet Labs
in downtown Portland and golly, Puppet is a wonderful host.

The evening kicked off with some 5-min lightning talks on "Raise your voice".

Speakers included Pamela Ju (Sr Analyst), Hailee Kenney (Software Engineer), and 
Grace Andrews (Associate Technical Solutions Engineer, https://twitter.com/GraceEwuraEsi).
